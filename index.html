Methodology & Assumptions

  • Engine runs internally in nominal dollars (so indexed tax brackets, CPP/OAS, and fixed mortgage payments behave realistically), then every figure shown is deflated to today's purchasing power.
  • Federal + provincial tax brackets are approximate 2025 figures, indexed forward by your inflation assumption. High-income federal BPA claw-back is not modelled.
  • Corporate account: annual mark-to-market gain realization, blended provincial passive-income tax rate, RDTOH refund and Capital Dividend Account (CDA) tracked; payouts modelled as non-eligible dividends.
  • RESP withdrawals assumed tax-free to the household (education assistance payments taxed in the student's hands).
  • Not tax or financial advice — a planning approximation. Confirm key numbers with an accountant/advisor before acting.
